Lil Tracy

Lil Tracy, also known as Souljawitch, Yung Bruh, Sicko Tracy, Tracy Minaj, and Lil Vampire is a Virginian rapper and singer. Born Jazz Ishmael Butler, he made his start and has sustained a career on Soundcloud, and is known for his frequent collaborations with the late Lil Peep.
